# HR-Data-Analysis-Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition using Tableau Public to identify the key factors influencing employee turnover. The dashboard provides interactive visualizations and business insights that help HR professionals understand attrition patterns and make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention.

## Project Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition patterns using HR data.
- Identify the major factors contributing to employee turnover.
- Perform data cleaning and exploratory data analysis (EDA).
- Build an interactive Tableau dashboard with KPIs and visualizations.
- Generate business insights and actionable HR recommendations.

## Business Problem

Employee attrition is one of the biggest challenges for organizations because it increases recruitment and training costs, reduces productivity, and affects overall business performance. The goal of this project is to identify the factors responsible for employee attrition and provide insights that help HR teams improve employee retention.

##  Dataset Information

- **Dataset:** IBM HR Employee Attrition Dataset
- **Domain:** Human Resource Analytics
- **Records:** 1,470 Employees
- **Target Variable:** Attrition (Yes / No)

## Key Insights

- Research & Development department experiences the highest employee attrition.
- Employees working overtime show higher attrition than those who do not.
- Employee satisfaction and work-life balance influence employee retention.
- Salary distribution and years at company provide valuable retention insights.
- Business travel frequency impacts employee turnover.

## Business Recommendations

- Reduce excessive overtime.
- Improve employee engagement programs.
- Strengthen career development opportunities.
- Enhance work-life balance initiatives.
- Review compensation and employee benefits regularly.
- Focus retention strategies on high-risk departments and job roles.
